Carbon pollution is causing global warming and should be regulated immediately.

Reasons Why

  • The scientific consensus leaves no room for doubt.

    Several peer-reviewed articles are published every daywith more evidence that carbon pollution is a grave threat to climate stability and therefore to all of us.

  • There's a high risk that we're causing a worldwide catastrophe.

    Would you let your family drink water from a well that 9 out of 10 tests said was poisoned? Would you ridicule scientists who tried to raise the alarm about it?

  • The issue of global warming is like where smoking was 25 years ago.

    Just like the tobacco industry used to do, the fossil fuel industry pays "experts" to keep the truth hidden, so profits can roll for just a little while longer.

  • Even the oil companies are saying global warming is caused by people.

    If the CEOs of Shell Oil and Exxon-Mobil say it — not to mention the Pope, the President of the United States, and the national academies of science of every country that has one — where does that leave you?

  • Carbon pollution is causing acidification of the oceans.

    Some carbon pollution falls into the ocean, making it more acidic. This dissolves the calcium shells of plankton, the base of the ocean's entire food chain.

  • It's time to have Congressional hearings about the global warming "hoax."

    Either there's a conspiracy on the part of the world's scientists to destroy our economy, or there's a conspiracy by industry to put civilization at risk for a few extra years of profit.

  • The catastrophe will hit the world's poor first and hardest.

    Allowing tens or hundreds of millions of people to die from extreme weather events caused by our carbon pollution will put us among the greatest mass murderers of History.

  • The Bible tells us to be concerned about global warming.

    God appointed Mankind to be steward of this world (Genesis 1:28). What more important stewardship decision is there than addressing global warming?

Reasons Why Not

  • Science changes its mind all the time.

    Is fat good or bad for you? What about carbs or salt? Chances are that the alarm over global warming is another hysteria that will fade with time.

  • It's a conspiracy by scientists.

    Mainstream scientists are a tight-knit community controlling grants and publishing. This is the perfect recipe for reinforcing a false consensus on global warming.

  • It's a conspiracy by World Government proponents.

    Agreeing with the alarmists on global warming means giving more power — perhaps emergency powers — to our government, and even to world bodies like the UN.

  • Some scientists disagree with the consensus.

    Every scientific breakthrough is achieved by scientists who buck the conventional wisdom. The minority of scientists who are skeptics of the theory of human-caused global warming may well be right.

  • The people in charge don't seem too concerned about global warming.

    If an asteroid were hurtling towards Earth, our leaders would be making it a priority. In the case of global warming, we hear lip service but no real urgency.

  • Science doesn't really understand climage change very well.

    This means we shouldn't rush to conclusions and certainly not risk damaging our already fragile economy with overreactions to a problem that may turn out to be nothing.

  • There will be technological fixes to the problem of global warming.

    Research is being conducted into solutions like carbon capture and geo-engineering, as well as alternative energy. We just need to let innovation take its course.

  • The Bible says that we shouldn't hold Nature higher than God.

    Environmentalism has a tendency to become a form of idolatry, where the "rights" of animals and an inanimate planet are held in higher esteem than God (Romans: 1:25).

  • The idea that humans have the power to change Earth's climate is ridiculous.

    The climate is too big, and too complex, and humans are far too small and insignificant.
